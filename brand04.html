Anti Social Social Club Joins Honda for Ricer-Friendly Collaboration

No VTEC logos to be found, however.

Nov 2, 2018 / By Jake Silbert

Anti Social Social Club returns for November with a collaborative capsule released in partnership with legacy car manufacturer Honda. 

The Japanese automaker’s “H” logo is emblazoned on a hoodie, T-shirt and cap, accompanied by the familiar ASSC branding on the opposite side of each item. The sole exception is a black hoodie emblazoned with a white and red “TYPE R,” just in time for the recently revealed 2019 Honda Civic Type R. 

The collaborative capsule is in conjunction with the brand’s latest Fall/Winter 2018 collection, viewable on the Anti Social Social Club Instagram page. Stay tuned to see what further comes of the collaborative relationship. The Honda-branded collection drops via Anti Social Social Club’s web store on November 3 at 8 a.m. PST. 

Meanwhile, a bone stock Acura Integra Type R from 1997 recently just sold for a record-breaking $63,800 USD.
