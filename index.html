							 Can I use Care-IT?
							 
    
								 
    Care-IT App can be downloaded for FREE on different OS. (E.g. Google Play Store & IOS App Store)
    
								 
    Care-IT is available for everyone, premium version of health report is under development and will be launched soon.
    
							 
    
						 
    • 

						 
  • 
							 What if my mum/dad doesn’t use smartphone?
							 
    
								 
    No worries! Your parents just need a smartwatch. Application can be downloaded by you to monitor their health status.
    
							 
    
						 
    • 

						 
  • 
							 What devices can the app be applied to?
							 
    
								 
    Our App and service can be applied to smartwatch, smartphone and can be integrated with any form of IoT. (E.g. Blood Pressure monitor or Blood Sugar Monitor) 
    
							 
    
						 
    • 

						 
  • 
							 How can I locate my parents with this app?
							 
    
								 
    We will utilize the device build-in GPS function to locate them. With Augmented Reality (AR) technology, we can further predict and visualize the location of the elderly.
    
							 
    
						 
    • 

						 
  • 
							 What is the service hour?
							 
    
								 
    Our App and cloud platform is avaliable 24/7.
    
							 
    
						 
    • 

						 
  • 
							 Where will my data be stored in?
							 
    
								 
    The data will be stored in a encrypted private cloud platform.
    
							 
    
						 
    • 
						 
  • 
							 Is the analysis accurate?
							 
    
							 
    Profile will be generated according to users’ data. Machine learning, clustering and classification techniques will be used for further training for the model. The longer you use the application, more data could be collected and analysed. You could also input your medical history to have a more precise result.
Environmental factors, such as temperature and humidity, would be also taken into account. Seasonality will also be take into account. For example, cardiovascular disease would be easier to be triggered in winter which is proved by various academic study worldwide.

						   Why do you have two options for siblings to contact their parents?
						   
    
						   
    The video call function can only be activated when a camera is installed at home. Users can choose whether to install it or not.
